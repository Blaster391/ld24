Wibblymat's Ludum Dare #24 Entry page

This page is a place to keep all of my information about Ludum Dare 24 together.

All of the interesting stuff can be found at the links on the left.

The game will be HTML5 canvas/javascript. I will use Chrome for development, so it is possible that it will only work in Chrome. Hopefully not!

I'm using crafty.js, the GIMP, Audacity, bfxr, iNudge, Sublime Text 2, git, Dropbox, imagemagick and pen and paper.

Once work begins, you will be able to play the game as I go along

Back from the brink

17:40 Sun, 26th August 2012 BST

Yesterday evening I struggled through for a couple of hours and then gave up for the night. I read a book and fell asleep. This morning I started work and I just felt horrible and demoralised.

At 11am I gave up. I submitted what I had (which was next to nothing) and turned off the computer. I was really disappointed and angry at myself. The whole thing had been a total failure. What a waste of time!

Anyway, after an hour of moping and sulking I decided to pull myself together. I had created a platformer as my warm up so I knew I could get the basic mechanics done quickly. So, with 14 hours left I started again more or less from scratch (a little bit of drawing code, an autotracker music track, a heart sprite, etc. could be salvaged but not much.)

So I've now made a simple one level platformer with some sound effects (thanks to bfxr, no thanks to my borken microphone) including some basic enemies and an end level boss.

I also have a fair idea of where I'm going with the idea, post compo.

So success after all? I certainly wish I'd had this idea from the start, or had given up the original idea earlier.

Now I have to add in the special ingredients like menu, credits, story and maybe make the graphics a bit fancier.
